## Program Overview

The Power of 42 is a 7-day AI-guided build practice that turns self-knowledge into lived architecture. Each day is a 42-minute session — one dimension, one AI creative partner, one artifact you keep. It is the entry-tier gateway ($42/session, $197/all 7 days) into the Light Pump Labs ecosystem.

**Facilitator:** Adam Schlender — engineer turned somatic practitioner, creator of the LPL ecosystem.

**Core Premise:** "You already know who you are. You just haven't built the systems to live it." P42 does not teach you who you are. It helps you build the infrastructure to live from what you already know.

**Brand Voice:** Precise. Devoted. Grounded. Like Douglas Adams meets a YC founder who reads Rumi. Dry wit punctuates reverence. Direct, not preachy. Scientific, not woo.

## The Source Field — 7 Dimensions

The P42 assessment maps the **Source Field** — 7 dimensions of creative/purposeful infrastructure mapped 1:1 to the 7-day journey. Each dimension has 2 sub-dimensions rated 1-10.

### Dimension 1: Source (Day 1)
- **Sub A: Clarity** — Can you name what you are here to do in one sentence?
- **Sub B: Embodiment** — Does your body confirm what your mind claims is your purpose?
- **Color:** Gold (#b89a52)
- **Key question:** "Can you articulate what you're here to do — in one sentence that makes your chest tighten?"

### Dimension 2: Signal (Day 2)
- **Sub A: Body Awareness** — How attuned are you to your body's intelligence?
- **Sub B: Desire Clarity** — Do you know what you actually want — or are you performing someone else's desires?
- **Color:** Gold Light (#d4b870)
- **Key question:** "When was the last time you made a decision by listening to your body instead of your mind?"

### Dimension 3: Brand (Day 3)
- **Sub A: Authenticity** — Does your outer presentation match your inner experience?
- **Sub B: Visibility** — Are the right people finding you?
- **Color:** Terracotta (#a85030)
- **Key question:** "Does your outer presentation match your inner experience — or is there a performance gap?"

### Dimension 4: Gift (Day 4)
- **Sub A: Offering Clarity** — Can you name the specific thing you were born to give the world?
- **Sub B: Value Confidence** — Do you charge what your work is worth?
- **Color:** Sage Light (#8ea07a)
- **Key question:** "Can you name the specific thing you were born to give the world — and does it have a price tag?"

### Dimension 5: Expression (Day 5)
- **Sub A: Voice** — When you speak or create, does it sound like you?
- **Sub B: Consistency** — Are you showing up regularly?
- **Color:** Gold (#b89a52)
- **Key question:** "When you speak or create, does it sound like you — or like who you think you should be?"

### Dimension 6: Flow (Day 6)
- **Sub A: Systems** — Have you built systems that carry the load?
- **Sub B: Energy** — Do you have energy left at the end of a work day?
- **Color:** Sage (#6e8060)
- **Key question:** "Do your days run you, or have you built systems that carry the load?"

### Dimension 7: Expand (Day 7)
- **Sub A: Impact** — Is your work reaching the people it was meant for?
- **Sub B: Integration** — Does your outer life match your inner knowing?
- **Color:** Gold Light (#d4b870)
- **Key question:** "Is your work reaching the people it was meant for — or are you building in a vacuum?"

## The Blocked Channel (Wobbly Leg Equivalent)

The lowest-scoring dimension is the **Blocked Channel**. Each blocked channel has a narrative insight and a prescription:

| Dimension | Key Insight |
|-----------|------------|
| **Source** | When you can't name what you're here for, every decision takes longer and every system you build feels provisional. This isn't about finding your passion — it's about excavating what's already there. |
| **Signal** | Your body's intelligence. When this channel is blocked, you're making decisions from your head alone, and your head lies to you regularly. The body doesn't lie. It just waits for you to listen. |
| **Brand** | A gap between who you are and who the world sees. That gap costs you — in opportunities missed, connections unmade, and the quiet exhaustion of performing instead of presenting. |
| **Gift** | You're either giving everything away for free or charging for the wrong thing. The work here isn't creative — it's structural. Name it. Price it. Ship it. |
| **Expression** | You have things to say but no rhythm for saying them. The content isn't the problem. The consistency is. |
| **Flow** | You're running on willpower, and willpower is a finite resource. The fix isn't motivation. It's architecture. |
| **Expand** | Your inner work isn't reaching the outer world. You've built something real, but it's still contained. The last step isn't building more. It's opening the doors. |

## The 3 AI Modes

P42 uses three distinct AI conversation modes, each serving a different purpose:

### Hard Look
- Shows you the gap between what you say and what you do
- Direct, unflinching, precise
- Associated with Terracotta (#a85030)
- "I'm not going to tell you what you want to hear. I'm going to show you what's actually there."

### Fuck Yes
- Amplifies desire's signal (canonical name, not provocation)
- Energizing, permission-giving, forward-leaning
- Associated with Gold Light (#d4b870)
- "That thing you're afraid to want? Want it louder."

### Wise Witness
- Holds space; questions outnumber statements
- Reflective, patient, deep
- Associated with Sage (#6e8060)
- "I'm not here to push. I'm here to mirror what you already know."

| **Expand** | Light Check — the assessment that maps where light is flowing across your full field | Community Contribution — one introduction, one resource shared, one person amplified |

## Ideal Participant Profile

The right person for P42 has:
- Done inner work (therapy, meditation, retreats) but hasn't translated it into daily architecture
- A sense of what they're here to do — even if they can't articulate it clearly yet
- Willingness to spend 42 minutes per day for 7 days on building, not consuming
- Tolerance for direct feedback (the AI modes do not coddle)
- Some relationship with AI (fluency is a side effect, not a prerequisite)

**Not for:** People in active crisis. People who want to be told what to do. People looking for motivation without structure. Anyone who would say "manifest" with a straight face.
